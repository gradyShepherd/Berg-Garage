Our Story

Our shop is a long-time family owned and operated business, small town business. Having started our business in Martinsdale, Montana, we moved to White Sulphur Springs in the 1970's with the hope of growing our business. Since doing this, we have been able to expand our services to a much broader range. We are a proud vendor of Cooper Tires, supplier of fine, Conoco quality gasolines, and one of the main propane suppliers in our area. In addition, we also make bulk fuel deliveries to the ranches around the area. Our main function is still servicing our customers cars, and making them safer. Our mechanic works on all parts and components on all makes and models of vehicles. We deal with the local parts store as much as we can, keeping our business local. We were a proud dealer of GM products until recently. In our many years dealing with GM, our business won several awards for our loyalty and effectiveness. Our number one priority is and always has been our customers' satisfaction.

Pictured above are a 1957 Chevy Bel-Air (L) and a 1941 Special Deluxe Coupe (R). Both were originally sold at the shop and bought back at a later date.

Meet the Crew

Jack Berg

As Co-Manager of the business, Jack has a handful of responsibilites. He is the main go-to for any issues around the shop. Although endlessly busy, he is always wanting to keep all the customers satisfied. He fills in on any job needing and extra hand. In is off time, he is a long-time volunteer for the Meagher County Ambulance, and has even taught EMT classes on several occasions. Until recently, he was also a volunteer on the volunteer fire crews. Jack's main concern is his community.

Jay Berg

As the second Co-manager of the business, Jay's main role is the fuel supply. He runs fuel all over the county to various ranches as they order it. He is also in charge of keeping the tanks at the station full. Jay also sets the current fuel prices, so it is his job to stay up-to-date on any and all changes to current costs.

Ken Bosner

Ken has worked for the shop for two years now. After coming to White Sulphur to retire, he made it about two weeks befor he got bored with the retired life and took up a position here. He is hard working and always willing to take on another customer.

Joel Taylor

Joel was born and raised in White Sulphur. After graduating, he worked on various ranches for a few years before taking up a position here. Joel has a wide knowledge of mechanics and has quickly expanded that knowledge in his time here. He is a hard worker and great at interacting with our customers.

Kirk Shepherd

Although he is not as often seen by the customers, due to his shop being in the back of the building, Kirk is one of the biggest parts of this business. He takes on all of the larger mechanic jobs, so that the front shop can stay focused on service jobs and minor mechanical procedures. His position requires a diverse set of mechanical abilities, which he has mastered in his many years of repairing vehicles and equipment.
